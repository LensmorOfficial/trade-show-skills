# Post-Show Skills

Skills for the follow-up phase after a trade show.

## Available Skills

### [post-show-followup](../post-show-followup/)

**Stage**: Post-Show · **Category**: Lead Conversion

Create tiered follow-up email sequences to convert booth conversations into sales opportunities. Generates hot / warm / cold lead classification criteria and multi-email sequences with different angles for each tier.

**When to use**: Within 24–48 hours after the show ends — the follow-up window closes fast.

---

## Typical Post-Show Workflow

```
Export leads from badge scanner / CRM
     ↓
post-show-followup → classify leads and generate sequences
     ↓
Load into HubSpot / Salesforce with merge tags → send
```

---

## The 48-Hour Rule

Industry data consistently shows that follow-up sent within 48 hours of a show closing gets 3–5× higher response rates than follow-up sent a week later. Contacts still remember the conversation; their inbox hasn't moved on yet.

Use `post-show-followup` immediately after returning from the show floor — not after you've unpacked, caught up on email, and filed your expense report.

---

## Connecting Pre-Show and Post-Show

The quality of post-show follow-up depends on pre-show preparation:

- **trade-show-budget-planner** sets CPL targets — use them to prioritize which leads get the most effort
- **booth-invitation-writer** sequences mean invited contacts already have context — reference it in your follow-up

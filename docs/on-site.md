# On-Site Skills (Roadmap)

Skills for use during a trade show — lead capture, qualification, and competitive intelligence.

> **Status**: Planned. No on-site skills are published yet.

## Planned Skills

### badge-qualifier *(planned)*

Real-time lead qualification at the booth. Takes a quick conversation summary or badge scan note and scores the lead (hot / warm / cold) based on ICP criteria you define.

**Planned stage**: On-Site · **Planned category**: Lead Qualification

---

### competitor-radar *(planned)*

Live competitive intelligence during the show. Track competitor booth activity, messaging changes, new product announcements, and hall positioning as you walk the floor.

**Planned stage**: On-Site · **Planned category**: Competitive Intelligence

---

## Why On-Site Skills Matter

Most trade show ROI is determined in the 48 hours after the show — but the quality of that follow-up depends entirely on what was captured on the floor. On-site skills close the gap between booth conversations and CRM-ready lead data.
